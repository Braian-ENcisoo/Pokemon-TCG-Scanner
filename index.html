2

Set width to 800 and height to 600. Click Refresh current page, then turn on Start Virtual Camera in OBS settings.

Open the app, enter your Set name, select the same camera used in OBS, and click START LIVE to begin.

FAQS

Does it work with any camera?

Yes, as long as your camera is recognized by your computer and has a decent focus, the scanner will work perfectly.

Is it free to use?

Yes, our scanner is completely free for all streamers to use during their live sessions.

Will this slow down my stream?

No, the app is lightweight and uses optimized processing, ensuring it won't impact your CPU or stream performance.

What should I do if a card is not recognized?

Ensure there is good lighting on the card and that it is placed flat in front of the camera. If issues persist, make sure your camera focus is sharp.
